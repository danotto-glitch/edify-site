About

Before Edify, our founder spent years doing growth strategy at one of the world's largest marketing groups — building audience frameworks and growth models for major consumer brands, a leading global tech company, and retail partners like Amazon and Walmart.

That range of experience is what makes the methodology work. It's not just about one industry — it's about understanding how consumers behave, where growth hides, and how to find it with data. Most mid-market companies haven't had access to that kind of thinking. Edify changes that.
